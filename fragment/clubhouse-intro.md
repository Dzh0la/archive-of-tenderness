---
title: In Clubhouse, asking for a name
tags: [memory, intimacy, hesitation]
---

He didn’t want to say it.  
I asked him about his name — Eфим —  
and he curled back into silence.

But then:  
“Let’s do it together,” he said.

So I did.  
I spoke first.  
And he followed.  
Tentatively.  
But he did.
